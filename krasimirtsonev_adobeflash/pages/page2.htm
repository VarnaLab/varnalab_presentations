История

1980 - Jonathan Gay почва да мисли за flash докато е в гимназията

1993 - Charlie Jackson, Jonathan Gay, и Michelle Welsh създават FutureWave Software и своя пръв продукт SmartSketch

1995 - добавят frame-by-frame animation - създават FutureSplash Animator за Macintosh и PC

1996 - Macromedia купува техния software и създава Flash 1.0 (малко преди това FutureWave предлага продукта си на Adobe, но те отказват да го купят)

1997 - Macromedia Flash 2 - Support of stereo sound, enhanced bitmap integration, buttons, the Library, and the capability to tween color changes

1998 - Macromedia Flash 3 - Brought improvements to animation, playback, and publishing, as well as the introduction of simple script commands for interactivity

1999 - Macromedia Flash 4 - Achieved 100 million installations of the Flash Player

2000 - Macromedia Flash 5 - ActionScript 1.0

2002 - Macromedia Flash MX / flash player 6 - support for video, application components, shared libraries, and accessibility

2003 - Flash MX 2004 / flash player 7 - ActionScript 2.0

2005 - Macromedia Flash 8

2006 - Adobe Flash Player 9 (2007 - за Linux)

2007 - Adobe Flash CS3 - ActionScript 3.0

2009 (February) - Adobe Integrated Runtime (AIR)
